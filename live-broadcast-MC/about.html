About Us

Mount Carmel Ministries International is a Christian ministry based in Sri Lanka. We are committed to building God’s kingdom in Sri Lanka and internationally by: evangelism and outreach church planting training for leadership and ministry We want you to enjoy viewing this website and finding out more about the work that we do and how you can be involved with us. We hope to hear from you soon.

Mount Carmel Church

We are committed to planting churches and releasing effective local ministry. At the moment we are working in several locations. Our main church is loacted in Bellwood. We are also in partnership internationally with a number of churches that share our heart and vision for effective ministry. Many of these are now close personal friends that we value greatly. These include: France, Germany, Norway, Italy, Switzerland, Australia, Malaysia and various parts of the UK.

Mount Carmel Theological College

The College started as the School of Theology and Ministry (STM) in September 1996 changing to College of Theology and Ministry (CTM) in 1997. We operated at these venues: Lighthouse Church, Rajawella and Lighthouse Church, Galaha. The College, with our motto, “Gentle in manner, resolute in execution” does not raise funds through any means. The main income is tea that is grown by the students themselves on 6 acres of land. On top of tea, the College grows Cinnamon, Cardamom, Cloves, Green and black Pepper. We are committed to emphasizing the need of both the academics and the anointing of the Holy Spirit. So, we are a College that offers solid education but with a great Pentecostal emphasis. Check our Mount Carmel Theological College page for more info.
