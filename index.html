What is Mount Carmel Ministries

About us

Mount Carmel Ministries International is a Christian ministry based in Sri Lanka. We are committed to building God’s kingdom in Sri Lanka and internationally by: evangelism and outreach church planting training for leadership and ministry. We want you to enjoy viewing this website and finding out more about the work that we do and how you can be involved with us.

Learn More

Mount Carmel Church

Main Church: Located in Bellwood

We are committed to planting churches and releasing effective local ministry. We are also in partnership internationally with a number of churches that share our heart and vision for effective ministry. Many of these are now close personal friends that we value greatly. These include: France, Germany, Norway, Italy, Switzerland, Australia, Malaysia and various parts of the UK.

Mount Carmel Theological College

The College started as the School of Theology and Ministry (STM) in September 1996 changing to College of Theology and Ministry (CTM) in 1997. We operated at these venues: Lighthouse Church, Rajawella and Lighthouse Church, Galaha.
